# Product Context

## Why This Exists
School district literacy leaders often work in isolation. A curriculum director in rural Mississippi faces similar challenges to one in urban Chicago, but they have no way to find each other. This platform solves that by matching leaders based on what actually matters: shared challenges and similar district demographics.

## Problems It Solves
- Isolation of district-level literacy leaders
- No structured way to find peers with similar challenges
- Lack of resource sharing across districts
- No community for evidence-based literacy leadership

## How It Works
1. Member creates profile, selects district (auto-populated from NCES data), picks literacy challenges
2. Matching algorithm scores compatibility (0-100) using 6 weighted criteria
3. Members browse matches, message each other, share resources
4. Moderators maintain community health via flagging/review system

## User Experience Goals
- Onboarding in under 2 minutes
- Matches feel relevant (shared challenges weighted highest at 45/100 pts)
- Messaging is simple and direct
- Moderation is transparent with audit trail
